About Me

Hey there! I'm Adin, a Computer Science student at Drexel University.

I love to learn and apply what I've learned through projects and other media. In classes, I maximize my opportunities for growth by tackling big projects, and I work hard to find elegant and efficient solutions.

I found my interest in computer science as a freshman in high school when a close friend of mine was teaching me the material he learned in his AP Computer Science class. While I had always been math-oriented and algorithmically-inclined, I hadn't encountered programming just yet - and I was in for a treat! After continuing to learn Java with my friend, I found my way into a data analysis position at the Children's Hospital of Phildelphia. I had the opportunity to learn MatLab in a professional research setting, and the scope and scale of the impact of programming began to set in. I was hooked, and haven't looked back since!

I have many blessings in my life - chiefly among them are my family, my friends, and my communities.
